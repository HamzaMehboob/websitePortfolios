# Pulse SaaS — Research

## Problem

Creators managing revenue across platforms need one dashboard to see KPIs, spot trends, and drill into campaign performance without spreadsheet exports.

## Persona

**Maya** — mid-size YouTube creator, 250K subscribers, runs 2–3 sponsored campaigns per month.

## User flows

1. **Morning overview** — Open dashboard → scan KPI row → check revenue trend → note audience dip.
2. **Campaign drill-down** — Filter campaigns table by status → sort by revenue → open row for detail.
3. **Quick navigation** — Press ⌘K → jump to Audience or Settings without mouse.

## Competitive audit

| Product | Strength | Gap Pulse addresses |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Stripe Dashboard | Clear revenue KPIs | Not creator/campaign-centric |
| Google Analytics | Deep audience data | Poor campaign sponsorship UX |
