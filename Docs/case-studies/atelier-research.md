# Atelier North — Research

## Problem

Premium brands need a studio site that communicates craft and restraint — not template agency noise.

## Persona

**Elena** — CMO at a lifestyle brand seeking a boutique partner for rebrand + digital launch.

## User flows

1. **Discover** — Land on home → scroll narrative → understand studio POV → click Work.
2. **View work** — Filter gallery by category → open project modal → read case excerpt.
3. **Contact** — About page → Contact CTA → submit inquiry (mock).

## Competitive audit

| Site | Strength | Gap |
|------|----------|-----|
| Pentagram | Editorial typography | Less scroll storytelling for solo boutique |
| Locomotive-style agencies | Motion richness | Often hurts mobile readability |
