Performance is a UX feature. Readers abandon long-form content when layout shifts and hero images block interaction.

## Set budgets per route type

| Route | LCP target | JS budget |
|-------|------------|-----------|
| Home | < 2.0s | 80 KB |
| Article | < 2.5s | 40 KB |

## Images are the story

Editorial sites live on photography. Use responsive `srcset`, lazy-load below the fold, and reserve aspect-ratio boxes to kill CLS.

### Font loading

Subset display faces. Preload only the weights used above the fold. System-ui fallbacks should match metrics closely enough to avoid jarring swaps.

## Islands, not waterfalls

Hydrate reading affordances — progress, TOC highlights — as small islands. Keep article body static HTML for crawlability and speed.

## What to measure weekly

Core Web Vitals at p75, plus **interaction readiness** on mobile mid-tier devices. Lab scores are direction; field data is truth.
